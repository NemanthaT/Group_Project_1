About Us

EDSA Lanka Consultancy

July 2, 2024 – Colombo, Sri Lanka – EDSA Lanka Consultancy is excited to announce its rebranding to Lanka Edu Solutions. This change reflects the company’s evolution and renewed focus on providing premier education, training, consulting, and research services in Sri Lanka and beyond.

Lanka Edu Solutions aims to continue its legacy of excellence by offering innovative programs and expert consulting services tailored to meet the diverse needs of individuals and organizations. The rebranding signifies our commitment to fostering knowledge and driving advancements across various sectors.

"Rebranding as Lanka Edu Solutions aligns with our vision of becoming a leading force in education and consultancy. We are dedicated to supporting our clients in achieving their goals and contributing to the development of a knowledgeable and innovative society."
