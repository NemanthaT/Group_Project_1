July 7, 2024 – Colombo, Sri Lanka – EDSA Lanka Consultancy is excited to announce its rebranding as Lanka Edu Solutions. This transition marks a renewed emphasis on innovation in education, training, and consulting services, strengthening its mission to address the growing challenges faced by organizations and research across Sri Lanka and beyond.

Lanka Edu Solutions aims to continue its legacy of providing unparalleled educational solutions to its clients. Through innovative practices and a client-focused approach, the organization is committed to driving excellence in the education and consultancy sectors.

"Rebranding as Lanka Edu Solutions aligns with our vision of growth and adaptability," stated the company spokesperson. "As a consultancy, we are dedicated to supporting our clients."
